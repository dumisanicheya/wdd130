Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a country located in Southern Africa, It attained independence on 18 April 1980. It is a landlocked country boardered by four countries namely Zambia in the north, South Africa in the south ,Botswana in the west and Mozambique in the East. It has a very good climate suitable for agriculture and vast wildlife. In it lies the mighty victoria falls which is one of the seven wonders of the world. Zimbabwe has a peacefull environment and christianity is the dominat religion constitung to 80 % of the population.
